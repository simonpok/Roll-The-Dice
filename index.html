1. Two players take turns rolling a dice
2. If a player rolls a 1, their turn ends, and the other player takes over.
3. If a player rolls a number between 2 and 6, that number is added to their current score for the turn.
4. If a player rolls a 1 on their turn, their current score becomes 0, and the other player takes over.
5. A player can choose to "hold" their current score, and it gets added to their total score. The turn then passes to the other player.
6. The game continues until one player reaches a total score of 50, at which point they win.
